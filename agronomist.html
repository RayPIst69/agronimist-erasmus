The platform

Discover consultancy

Farmers can find a consultant (Consultants can be Agronomists, Zootechnicians, veterinaries and Foresty engineers) at any time.

Technical support

Farmers can have real time support by request and the platform will send allerts to users with:

  • Weather threshold and plagues/diseases predictions (weather sources)
  • Vegetal indexes out of normal parameters (aerial images)
  • Animal threshold and geofencing (animal collar)
  • Emergence of crop plagues/diseases/fungus/threats (crop monitor)
  • Water stress and fertilizer use(irrigation systems)

Live Chat

Ask and send any data (images, video and other agro data) to consultant to receive his best advice to perform.

New income source

Any consuntant can have a new source of income by helping farmers without having travel costs. They can be in their home and earning money while giving tech support.

Integrations

Weather data

Integration of any source of weather data (weather stations, online services, etc.). This is showned to farmer and also to the consuntant.

Aerial images

Integration of sattelite images, upload from drones and/or planes and images interpretation and technical alerts. This is showned to farmer and also to the consuntant.

Crop monitor

1 - A trap that shows in pictures or video what kind of plagues are caught inside. Artificial Intelligence alerts will be sent to farmer saying which insect and how many there are helping to act faster and with the proper action.
2 - A camera that overview a crop and analyse it's development and the appereance of any crop threat (disease and/or fungus or big animal).

Irrigation systems

Integration of any irrigation system. This is showned to farmer and also to the consuntant.

Animal collar

Integration of the sensor that shows where animals are, their weel being, temperature measurement, animal heat, death/disappereance, etc. This is showned to farmer and also to the consuntant.

Farm management software

Integration of a farm management software to help farmers to manage all daily information.
